### Project Title
    ** Bikeshare Project

### Description
    ** Users can explore the bikeshare data from three cities;
        - new_york_city
        - washington
        - chicago
    ** search data based on;
        - name of the month
        - name of the day of the week
    ** and provide followings;
        - the most frequent times of travel given selected data
        - the most popular month given selected data
        - the most popular day of the week given selected data
        - the most popular start hour given selected data
        - statistics on the most popular stations and trip
            - the most popular start station given selected data
            - the most popular end station given selected data
